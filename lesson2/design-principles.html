PARC: Repetition

Healthline healthline.com

Healthline's home page is an excellent example of the Repetition Principle. We can observe how consistent they are, using the same imagery style throughout the page. This consistency creates a strong sense of identity, turning the user experience more enjoyable.

Visual Hierarchy

W3schools w3schools.com

I chose the W3schools website to illustrate the Visual Hierarchy Principle because I think they did a good job separating their home page content. Though there is a lot of content on the page, you can visualize the more important ones: they appear first and have more (and bigger) call to action buttons.

White Space

Coolors coolors.co

Coolors.co has a straight-to-the-point, clean design. There is no clutter. You can clearly read the content of the page. The use of white space makes you focus on what they want to advertise and makes it impossible to get lost on their website.
